# Ethical Code

- Don't be a dong.
- Treat everyone with dignity and respect.
- Marginalized Lives Matter; because
  - Trans lives are equal to Black lives are equal Indigenous lives are equal to Brown lives are equal to Women's lives are equal to ND lives are equal to disabled lives are equal to improverished lives are equal to childrens rights. They all matter and they/we are all victims of the same (colonial) system.
  - none of us are free until all marginalized peoples are treated as equals with equal rights and access. There is no hierarchy. Only kyriarchy.
- No one deserves to die.
- Everyone deserves bodily autonomy, dignity, food, safety, security, and shelter.
- Everyone has intrinsic value.
- Everyone needs to decolonize themselves.
- The world needs actual stewardship of land, air and water by those who intend to protect it.
- The same goes for all that happens on those lands. The world needs actual stewardship of the needs of the many.
- Only universal justice is justice. Because exceptions in justice are inherently injust. Not all power dynamics are applicable 100% of the time. Only foundational rules that account for "exceptions" are truly just.
- We are all temporary guests of "Mother Earth"/this planet. We need to treat the planet that way.
